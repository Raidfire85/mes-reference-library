Author: MeridiusIX

Modular Encounters Spawner - Random Grid Name Generator

The Modular Encounter Spawner has an option available to it to randomize the name of your SpawnGroup's prefabs (via the UseRandomNameGenerator tag), to give a unique element to your encounters. This is achieved by providing a pattern in your SpawnGroup setup and then the Name Generator will look for keywords to replace in that pattern.

Example: A provided pattern of The GoodAdjective ExplorerNoun could result in The Gilded Courier, The Heroic Navigator, etc.

The list of different words is far too large to maintain on this page, so I will instead list some of the keywords (in bold) used in the Generator, along with some example results, so you can easily create patterns.

  • RandomLetter: A, B, C, etc

  • RandomNumber: 1, 2, 3, etc

  • RandomChar: A, 1, B, etc

  • GoodAdjective: Brave, Kind, Patriotic, etc

  • NeutralAdjective: Cautious, Stubborn, Lonely, etc

  • BadAdjective: Dishonorable, Unwelcome, Wicked, etc

  • FunnyAdjective: Wacky, Lewd, Derpy, etc

  • ColorAdjective: Red, Green, Blue, etc

  • GoodNoun: Victory, Morality, Integrity, etc

  • NeutralNoun: Caution, Resignation, Persuasion, etc

  • BadNoun: Malice, Spite, Suffering, etc

  • FunnyNoun: Trickery, Dirty-Laundry, Private-Stash, etc

  • AuthorityNoun: Ambassador, Monarch, Minister, etc

  • MilitaryNoun: Officer, Captain, Major, etc

  • BaddieNoun: Scoundrel, Infiltrator, Malcontent, etc

  • ExplorerNoun: Voyager, Adventurer, Prospector, etc

  • JobNoun: Courier, Merchant, Doctor, etc

  • BirdNoun: Pigeon, Eagle, Vulture, etc

  • AnimalNoun: Buffalo, Coyote, Badger, etc

  • FishNoun: Whale, Lobster, Remora, etc

  • InsectNoun: Dragonfly, Grasshopper, Mosquito, etc

  • SurnamesNoun: Wilson, Armstrong, Lancaster, etc

  • NatoLetter: Alpha, Bravo, Charlie, etc

  • GreekLetter: Alpha, Beta, Gamma, etc
